1. 音形义讲义 (The Glossary)

学习下列核心词汇，点击 🔊 图标收听发音，注意词性和例句。

entrepreneur /ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːr/ n.
原文：People often wonder why some entrepreneurs have greater success than others.
The young entrepreneur started his own business immediately after graduating from university.
这位年轻的企业家大学毕业后立即创办了自己的公司。
ethic /ˈeθɪk/ n.
原文：Is it habits, connections, luck, work ethic or any other behavior?
A strong work ethic is often considered more important than talent in achieving long-term success.
在取得长期成功方面，强烈的职业道德通常被认为比天赋更重要。
determination /dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/ n.
原文：It is a strong determination that allows you to do something difficult.
Her determination to win the competition impressed all the judges.
她赢得比赛的决心给所有评委留下了深刻印象。
discipline /ˈdɪsəplɪn/ n.
原文：People with a great amount of willpower have the discipline to develop positive, successful habits.
Self-discipline is essential for anyone who wants to master a new skill without supervision.
对于任何想要在无人监督下掌握新技能的人来说，自律是必不可少的。
incredible /ɪnˈkredəbl/ adj.
原文：Even with an incredible amount of talent, without the discipline... it can be difficult to achieve success.
The view from the top of the mountain was absolutely incredible after the long hike.
经过长途跋涉，山顶的景色简直令人难以置信。
critical /ˈkrɪtɪkl/ adj.
原文：It is critical to create good habits and take the actions necessary to stick to those habits.
It is critical that we address these environmental issues before it is too late.
至关重要的是，我们要在为时已晚之前解决这些环境问题。
automatically /ˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli/ adv.
原文：...you will automatically perform those tasks on a day-to-day basis.
The doors of the shopping mall open automatically when you approach them.
当你走近时，购物中心的门会自动打开。
passionate /ˈpæʃənət/ adj.
原文：...or if your business is something you're passionate about.
She is deeply passionate about wildlife conservation and environmental protection.
她对野生动物保护和环境保护充满热情。
associated /əˈsoʊsieɪtɪd/ adj./v.
原文：Having a valuable outcome associated with a habit will help you stick to that habit permanently.
There are many health risks associated with smoking cigarettes and heavy drinking.
吸烟和酗酒有许多相关的健康风险。
permanently /ˈpɜːrmənəntli/ adv.
原文：...help you stick to that habit permanently.
The exhibition has been permanently closed due to lack of funding from the government.
由于缺乏政府资助，该展览已永久关闭。
