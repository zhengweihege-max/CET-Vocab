1. 音形义讲义 (The Glossary)

学习下列核心词汇，注意发音、词性和例句。

allegedly /əˈledʒɪdli/ adv.
原文：A woman was charged with allegedly violating a Rhode Island city law against feeding wild animals.
The company was allegedly involved in a financial scandal last year.
这家公司据称去年卷入了一起财务丑闻。
violate /ˈvaɪəleɪt/ v.
原文：A woman was charged with allegedly violating a Rhode Island city law against feeding wild animals.
Students who violate the university's code of conduct may face serious disciplinary actions.
违反大学行为准则的学生可能会面临严重的纪律处分。
charge /tʃɑːrdʒ/ v.
原文：A woman was charged with allegedly violating a Rhode Island city law against feeding wild animals.
He was charged with theft after being caught stealing from the store.
他在被抓到从商店偷窃后被指控盗窃。
capture /ˈkæptʃər/ v.
原文：Newly installed cameras captured several rats active in the middle of the day.
The photographer managed to capture the beautiful sunrise over the mountains.
摄影师设法捕捉到了山脉上美丽的日出。
prohibit /prəˈhɪbɪt/ v.
原文：It is prohibited to feed any wild animals including birds in Rhode Island City.
Smoking is strictly prohibited in all public buildings.
所有公共建筑内严禁吸烟。
intention /ɪnˈtenʃn/ n.
原文：Nevertheless, while the woman's intention was to feed birds...
I accepted the job offer with the intention of gaining more experience.
我接受这份工作是为了获得更多经验。
administration /ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃn/ n.
原文：Anthony Moretti, director of the city administration, said he saw more than 20 rats near the woman's home.
The new school administration is planning to improve the library facilities.
新一届学校行政部门计划改善图书馆设施。
